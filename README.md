AI/ML engineer with four years of industry experience before an MSc in Computer Science & Engineering (AI) at the University of Oulu. I build retrieval-augmented and LLM systems and machine-learning pipelines in Python, PyTorch, and PySpark, on a foundation of shipping software and leading technical delivery.

### Highlights

- **Co-authored** the first publicly available dataset linking Finnish railway operations to synchronised weather data (~38.5M observations, 2018-2024); established an XGBoost baseline of 2.73-minute MAE for station-level delay prediction. [arXiv:2601.16592](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16592)
- **MSc thesis:** a retrieval-augmented assistant that turns corporate-learning data into business insight. Answer quality 82.7% (+13.6 points over the manual baseline); retrieval MRR 0.96, Precision@1 0.93. [Open-access thesis](https://oulurepo.oulu.fi/handle/10024/63278)
